Salary Negotiation Tips

Strategies to maximize your compensation

Research Market Rates

Use salary data to understand your worth in the market and set realistic expectations.

Set Your Target Range

Determine your ideal salary range and be prepared to negotiate within those boundaries.

Timing is Key

Wait for the right moment to discuss salary, usually after demonstrating your value.

Consider Total Package

Look beyond base salary to benefits, bonuses, equity, and other compensation elements.

Practice Your Pitch

Rehearse your negotiation points and be confident in articulating your value.

Be Willing to Walk Away

Know your bottom line and be prepared to decline if the offer doesn't meet your requirements.
