Practice Questions

Test your knowledge with common interview questions

Tell me about a time you faced a difficult challenge at work.

Tips:

  • Use the STAR method (Situation, Task, Action, Result)
  • Focus on your problem-solving approach
  • Highlight what you learned

Describe a situation where you had to work with a difficult team member.

Tips:

  • Focus on collaboration and communication
  • Show empathy and understanding
  • Demonstrate conflict resolution skills

Explain the difference between REST and GraphQL APIs.

Tips:

  • Compare data fetching approaches
  • Discuss over-fetching vs under-fetching
  • Mention use cases for each

How would you design a scalable database system?

Tips:

  • Consider read/write patterns
  • Discuss partitioning strategies
  • Mention caching and CDN

Why do you want to work for this company?

Tips:

  • Research the company thoroughly
  • Connect your values to company culture
  • Mention specific projects or initiatives

Where do you see yourself in 5 years?

Tips:

  • Show ambition but be realistic
  • Connect to the role you're applying for
  • Demonstrate growth mindset

How do you motivate team members who are underperforming?

Tips:

  • Focus on understanding root causes
  • Discuss coaching and mentoring approaches
  • Show empathy and support

Describe a time you had to make a difficult decision as a leader.

Tips:

  • Show your decision-making process
  • Discuss stakeholder considerations
  • Highlight the outcome and lessons learned

Interview Tips & Best Practices

Essential advice to ace your interviews

Research the Company

Learn about the company's mission, values, recent news, and the role you're applying for.

Prepare Your Stories

Have 3-5 specific examples ready that demonstrate your skills and achievements.

Practice Timing

Keep your answers concise (1-2 minutes) and practice with a timer.

Ask Questions

Prepare thoughtful questions about the role, team, and company culture.

Body Language

Maintain eye contact, sit up straight, and use confident gestures.

Follow Up

Send a thank-you email within 24 hours after the interview.
