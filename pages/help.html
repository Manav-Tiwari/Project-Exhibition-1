Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account on ResumeBERT?

Creating an account is simple! Click the "Sign Up" button on our homepage and fill in your basic information. You can also sign up using your Google or LinkedIn account for faster registration.

What information do I need to provide during registration?

We require your name, email address, and a password. You can also optionally provide your current job title and location to help us personalize your experience from the start.

How does the AI matching system work?

Our AI analyzes your skills, experience, and preferences to find the best job matches. It considers factors like required skills, experience level, location preferences, and company culture to provide personalized recommendations.

How do I upload my resume?

Go to your Profile page and click on the "Upload Resume" button. We support PDF, DOC, and DOCX formats. You can also copy and paste your resume content directly into our resume builder.

Can I edit my profile after creating it?

Yes! You can edit your profile at any time. Go to your Profile page and click the "Edit Profile" button. All changes are saved automatically, and you can update your information as often as needed.

How do I add skills to my profile?

In your Profile settings, go to the Skills section and click "Add Skill." You can type in your skills or select from our suggested skill list. You can also rate your proficiency level for each skill.

How do I search for jobs?

Use the search bar at the top of the Jobs page to search by keywords, company names, or skills. You can also use our advanced filters to narrow down results by location, job type, experience level, and salary range.

What does the match score mean?

The match score indicates how well a job aligns with your profile. It's calculated based on your skills, experience, preferences, and the job requirements. Higher scores mean better matches!

How do I save jobs for later?

Click the bookmark icon on any job listing to save it to your favorites. You can access all saved jobs from your Dashboard under the "Saved Jobs" section.

How do I apply for a job?

Click the "Apply Now" button on any job listing. You'll be prompted to upload your resume (if not already uploaded), write a cover letter, and confirm your application details.

Can I track my application status?

Yes! Go to your Applications page to see the status of all your applications. You'll receive email notifications when there are updates, and you can also check the timeline for each application.

How do I withdraw an application?

Go to your Applications page, find the application you want to withdraw, and click the "Withdraw" button. Note that you cannot withdraw an application after it has been reviewed by the employer.

How do I change my password?

Go to Settings > Privacy & Security > Account Security. Enter your current password and then your new password twice to confirm the change.

What's included in the Premium plan?

Premium includes unlimited job applications, advanced AI matching, priority support, detailed analytics, and access to exclusive job listings. You can upgrade anytime from your Account settings.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Go to Settings > Account > Subscription and click "Cancel Subscription." Your Premium features will remain active until the end of your current billing period.
