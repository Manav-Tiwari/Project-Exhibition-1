Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I reset my password?

Click on "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your email address, and follow the instructions sent to your inbox.

Can I change my email address?

Yes, you can update your email address in your profile settings. You'll need to verify the new email address before the change takes effect.

How do I delete my account?

Go to your account settings and select "Delete Account" at the bottom. Please note that this action cannot be undone.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers for annual plans.
